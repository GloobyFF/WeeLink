Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

Overview

WeeLink is a PC VR and desktop streaming app for Apple Vision Pro. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and how you can contact us.

WeeLink is designed primarily for local-network streaming between your Apple Vision Pro and your Windows PC.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not operate a user account system for WeeLink, and we do not intentionally collect or sell personal information through the app.

We do not collect your streamed video, audio, desktop contents, game contents, passwords, payment card numbers, or Apple ID password.

Information Stored Locally

WeeLink may store configuration data locally on your devices so the app can function properly, including:

  • Paired device or server information.
  • Connection settings, streaming quality preferences, and display settings.
  • Game library metadata, custom game entries, cover art settings, collections, and hotkey configuration.

This information is used to provide app features and is not sent to us unless you choose to share it for support.

Support Communications

If you contact us for support, we may receive the information you choose to provide, such as your email address, device model, operating system version, screenshots, logs, error messages, and a description of the issue.

We use this information only to respond to your request, troubleshoot problems, improve WeeLink, and maintain support records.

Purchases and Subscriptions

If WeeLink offers paid features, purchases or subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your full payment card details.

Apple may provide purchase status information needed to unlock features, manage subscriptions, restore purchases, or handle support requests. Apple's own privacy policy applies to App Store payment processing.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party platforms such as Apple App Store services, GitHub Pages, and GitHub Issues to distribute information, host this website, process app distribution, or provide support channels.

Those services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may disclose information if required by law, to protect our rights, to prevent abuse, or to provide support through services you choose to use.

Children's Privacy

WeeLink is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Your Choices

You may delete local app data by removing the app or clearing its settings where supported by the operating system. You may also contact us to request deletion of support emails or support records associated with your email address, subject to legal and operational requirements.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or need support, contact us at gloobyfzl@qq.com.
